DORIAN ELECTRA


About




Dorian Electra (born Dorian Electra Fridkin Gomberg, June 25, 1992) is an American singer, songwriter, video and performance artist. Dorian identifies as genderfluid and uses they/them pronouns.

Dorian’s songs blend 00s pop, 80s funk and metal elements with a unique and futuristic, gender-bending vocal style. In 2017 they along side refinary29 they created 4 music videos about the woman body, shameless sex and gender questions, in the same year they featured on Charli XCX’s track “Femmebot” along with Mykki Blanco and have played live with XCX at a number of shows and afterparties curated and organized by Dorian. Dorian’s on-stage persona is best described as a mix of Britney Spears and a sleazy Las Vegas lounge singer. Most of the themes in theirs songs are toxic masculinity, gender identity, lgbtqi+ rights and mental illeness.

In 2019 released their first studio album named "Flamboyant", a bright and vanguard pop album which gain traction from a lot of queer listeners making dorin gain a lot of atraction from the media. In 2020 released "my agenda" a queer themed album with a lot of metal and hardcore inspiration which gave Dorian even more atraction from the media and a lot of new fans.
